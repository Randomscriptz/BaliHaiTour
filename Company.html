About the company

Bali Hai Tours is a family-owned company that prides itself in offering the best of the best in island and wildlife exploration.

About our crew

  • Our captains and shipmates live on the island and have many years of experience in the ocean, as well as island and wildlife touring.
  • Our captains are licensed by the U.S. Coast Guard and all are Coast Guard Auxiliary members.
  • In addition all crew members have received:
    • First Aid, CPR & Lifesaving training from the Red Cross
    • Advanced Life saving from Aquatic Safety Management Services.
    • All have been certified in search & rescue and water safety.
      It's our highest priority.

Company Affiliations

  • D.B.A. Kauai Boat Enterprises
  • We repair all inflatable vessels on the island of Kauai
  • We are 4 star certified Zodiac sales and repair facility
  • We are the Mercury (boat motor) dealer for Kauai
  • Our boats are designated U.S. Coast Guard facilities for Search & Rescue and training
  • We also support all branches of the military with marine training
