Discovering an island by sea is an old Maritime tradition, as when long ago the first Tahitians arrived in the Hawaiian Islands by outrigger canoe. Later, they were followed by the British ships, Resolution and Discovery, which first dropped anchor in Kauai in 1778. Now as then, island exploration by boat is an Adventure of New Discovery. . .

Intimate Experience

Though our 20 foot Zodiac has 12 passenger capacity, we take no more than 6 guests per cruise, each participant can have a personal and intimate experience with the ocean and sea life, which can be no more than a few feet away. All tours include an opportunity to snorkel (gear provided at no extra charge), swim, snack (provided with drinks at no extra charge) or just sit back and enjoy the incredible scenery and wildlife around you.
Our helpful, friendly and knowledgeable crew are close by and always on hand to guide and inform. No loud speakers or microphones to separate you.
Weather and tides permitting, our smaller boat can get you into caves and closer to beaches than our larger competitors.
