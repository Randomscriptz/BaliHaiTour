Your List

As we said before... A towel and a smile is all you need to enjoy this adventure, but we know a few comfortable items of your own may enhance your experience.
Please feel free to bring...

  • Sun screen...
    the higher the number the better
    We recommend Banana Boat Sport
    It's water, wind and sweat proof.

  • Camera...bring extra memory and batteries
    NOTE: we cannot be responsible for any electronic equipment...
    cell phones, expensive cameras, etc.

  • Sun glasses and/or prescription glasses
    be sure to have a string or cord so they won't be lost

  • Hat
    be sure to have a string or cord so it won't be lost
    It can get windy if we have to head into the wind
    However, for ecological reasons, we will try to retrieve anything that falls off the boat.

  • Towels

  • Swim ware, if you plan to jump into the ocean
    Take note...there are NO changing rooms on the boat,
    though our departure locations have restrooms & fresh water showers

  • Dry clothes...
    though we'll ask you to lock them in your car prior to departure

  • Personal snacks or beverages,
    if you have special dietary needs or picky children
    BYOB is perfectly fine with us
