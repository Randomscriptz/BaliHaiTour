Our Warnings to you...

If you've recently injured yourself, have recently had surgery, are early trimester pregnancy or suffer from back problems, we care about your comfort and don't want you to be injured or further injured, therefore please heed the following...

  • You can be up close and personal with sea creatures.
    though we've never had a serious incident, your condition could be aggravated by quick movements on your or their part

  • Mother Nature can get cranky
    Waves may get high
    Rough (sometimes very rough) bouncing can occur

  • Our cruises are for the thrill seeker, looking for a unique adventure
    We will be going fast through the waves.
    Quick turns, splashing and a loud "Whoopee!" or two may occur

  • We don't want to tell anyone not to come
    Please use your best judgement
    You know your body better than anyone

  • Lastly, our boats are NOT wheelchair accessible
    However, with help from your caregiver, we can lift you (not the chair) into the boat
